Tally Prime with GST Course

Tally Prime is the absolute standard for business accounting in India. This hands-on program at I GROWW ACADEMY teaches you how to record, organize, and analyze business operations in Tally from scratch.

Cover key topics like ledger creation, inventory management, bank reconciliation, generating MIS reports, processing GST invoices, and exporting financial statements.

What You Will Learn

Every course is built around practical modules, guided assignments, and career-focused mentoring.

Learn practical skills. Build confidence. Launch your career with expert support.
Core Module 1

Create accounting masters, configure inventory groups, and reconcile monthly bank statements.

Core Module 2

Enable GST features, track tax credits, process payroll, and export Trial Balance & Balance Sheets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about course suitability, practical learning, and placement support.

  • Do I need accounting experience for Tally?
    No, our syllabus starts with standard double-entry bookkeeping rules before showing you Tally tools.
  • Will I learn GST integration in Tally?
    Yes! You will learn how to configure GST rates, create tax invoices, and prepare GSTR compliance summaries.
  • Are there project assignments?
    Yes! You will audit a simulated firm's complete financial transactions for a full fiscal year.
  • What placements can I get?
    Graduates are placed as Accounts Executives, Tally Operators, Billing Executives, and Audit Assistants.
